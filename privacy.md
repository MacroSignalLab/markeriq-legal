# MarkerIQ Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 12, 2026_

MarkerIQ is a personal health-tracking app for iPhone, built to be private by
default. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your
information.

## The short version

MarkerIQ stores your information only on your iPhone. It has no account or
login, no servers or backend, and no analytics, advertising, or tracking. Your
data is never uploaded or sent to us or to any third party. We do not have
access to your data and cannot see it.

## What MarkerIQ stores (on your device only)

Information you enter or import — biomarker readings, plan items, supplements,
fitness activities, health documents, and app preferences — is saved locally on
your iPhone and stays on your device.

## Apple Health (optional, read-only)

If you choose to connect Apple Health, MarkerIQ can read the health data types
you permit (for example: nutrition, weight, height, waist circumference, body
temperature, heart-rate variability, resting heart rate, blood pressure,
exercise minutes, and sleep). Data you allow may be saved to your biomarker
history on your device. MarkerIQ never writes to Apple Health and never uploads
Apple Health data. Connecting Apple Health is optional, and you can change or
revoke access at any time in the Apple Health app.

## Camera (optional)

If you choose to scan a lab report, MarkerIQ uses your camera and processes the
image on your device to recognize text. Scanned images and recognized text are
not uploaded. Imports are always shown to you for review before anything is
saved.

## No data collection, no tracking

MarkerIQ does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your data. It contains no
analytics or tracking technologies and does not use your information for
advertising. It does not send your health information anywhere.

## Your control and deletion

You can delete all locally stored MarkerIQ data at any time using
Settings → Privacy & Data → Reset all local data inside the app. Deleting the
app and its data removes locally stored MarkerIQ data from the device. Files you
export yourself (for example, a CSV) leave MarkerIQ's control once exported.

## Not medical advice

MarkerIQ is for personal tracking, organization, visualization, and education
only. It is not medical advice and does not diagnose, treat, prevent, or cure
any condition. Always consult a qualified clinician for medical decisions.

## Children

MarkerIQ is not directed to children and is intended for personal use by the
device owner.
