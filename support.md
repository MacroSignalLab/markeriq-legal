# MarkerIQ Support

MarkerIQ is a private, on-device health-tracking app for iPhone. Everything
stays on your device — there's no account to manage.

## Common questions

**How do I connect Apple Health?**
Settings → Apple Health → Connect. It's optional and read-only; MarkerIQ never
writes to Apple Health.

**Where is my data stored?**
Only on your iPhone. Nothing is uploaded.

**How do I delete everything?**
Settings → Privacy & Data → Reset all local data. Deleting the app and its data
removes locally stored MarkerIQ data from the device.

**Is this medical advice?**
No. MarkerIQ is for personal tracking and education only — consult a qualified
clinician for medical decisions.

**What iPhone do I need?**
An iPhone running iOS 17 or later.
